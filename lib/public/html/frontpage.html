Music Technology Project: Mapping survey for sonification application.

Welcome to this mapping test application for my music technology project. The purpose of this site is to aquire information regarding the spatialisation of sound under four different data to audio mappings. In short; four different ways of transfering (x, y) information into audio output.



Outline:

On the proceeding page is a canvas object that will generate sound when you click and drag within it.

The sound generated will depend upon the selected mapping, which can be changed using the dropdown.

Your goal is to listen to the sound produced by clicking the , then approximate the path used to generate that sound.

For example: A straight line from left to right on a spatialisation mapping (2) will produce a steady frequency panning from left to right.

Once you have identified and drawn the path produced within the audio playback, use the to submit your approximation.

Please try to submit as many paths as you can on as many mappings as possible, although any usage or feedback is appreciated.

When you are ready to begin, tick the box below and press continue.
